SGA Series

USB Vector
Signal Generator

SGA series is the high-performance USB vector signal generator from PN Technologies, designed with ultra-compact form factor (only 360 g) and high signal fidelity. Equipped with 16-bit DAC, SGA-60 covers frequency range from 9 kHz to 6 GHz, output power from −100 dBm to +14 dBm, and playback bandwidth of up to 100 MHz. Operating directly from USB-C power, it supports a wide variety of analog and digital modulations (including QPSK, QAM up to 1024 QAM, OFDM, and custom arb waves), providing researchers, field engineers, and system integrators with an incredibly agile, mobile signal generation solution.
