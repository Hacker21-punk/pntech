Key Features

Extended Frequency

NXE-200 reaches up to 20 GHz — ideal for 5G NR and satellite signal monitoring.

Network Distributed

Build distributed spectrum monitoring arrays over standard Ethernet infrastructure.

GNSS Integration

Every measurement is time-stamped and geolocated via built-in GNSS receiver.

Compact Form

At just 650 g and 167×117×28 mm, deploy virtually anywhere.

Real-Time Analysis

100 MHz real-time bandwidth captures fast hopping and burst signals.

Industrial Grade

Wide operating temperature for outdoor and industrial deployments.
