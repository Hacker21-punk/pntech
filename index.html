Products

PX-N90 9 GHz

Handheld
Real-Time Spectrum Analyzer

PX-N90 is the 9 GHz spectrum analyzer with tracking generator support (opt.). It also offers advanced measurement including AM/FM demodulation, channel power, ACPR for free.

SAN-60 6 GHz

USB
Spectrum Analyzer/Receiver

SAN-60 is the spectrum analyzer/receiver up to 6 GHz. It offers compact design and fast sweep for spectrum monitoring, drone detection and embedded RF systems.

IP68 RUGGED
Spectrum Analyzer
