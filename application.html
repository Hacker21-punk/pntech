Application Solutions

Precision RF instrumentation designed to solve complex test and measurement challenges in the field and in the laboratory.

Case Study 01

RF Signal Mapping and Geolocation

By combining high-speed real-time spectrum analyzers with integrated GPS/GNSS receivers, field engineers can perform comprehensive signal coverage audits and trace interference sources instantly. The SAStudio4 Mapping Mode visualizes signal intensity heatmaps on interactive GIS maps directly in the field.

  • GPS-Tagging Data Points: Log absolute coordinate and altitude data for every spectrum sweep.
  • Vibrant Heatmap Overlays: Draw color-coded signal strength contours directly over geographical layers.
  • Geolocation direction lines: Correlate multiple bearing measurements to pinpoint the exact locations of rogue emitters.
  • Offline Map Capabilities: Store and load local geographic maps for seamless outdoor audits without internet.

Case Study 02

Tracking Generator & Transmission Sweeps

Stimulus-response measurements are crucial for evaluating passive and active RF components. By linking the tracking generator capabilities of our SGA-60 vector signal generator with real-time spectrum analyzers, engineers can characterize duplexers, sweep antenna feedlines, and perform cable loss calibrations instantly in the field.

  • Cable Attenuation Calibrations: Standardize and normalize path losses to guarantee laboratory-grade accuracy.
  • Filter Insertion/Return Loss: Trace transfer characteristics, bandwidth cutoffs, and duplexer isolation curves.
  • Field Feeder Auditing: Test base station coax cables, adaptors, and antennas for structural defects.
  • Dynamic Range Performance: Achieve over 100 dB dynamic range sweeps for precise leakage audits.

Case Study 03

SDR++ & Third-Party SDR Software Support

Our instruments feature complete native compatibility with the leading open-source software-defined radio suite, SDR++. Combined with our multi-language software development kits (SDKs), researchers and developers can capture high-speed IQ streams, execute custom signal demodulations, and automate laboratory test flows.

  • Native SDR++ Integration: Run SDR++ natively on your host PC to demodulate and record RF signals.
  • Full-Bandwidth IQ Streaming: Stream ultra-low latency, phase-coherent complex IQ data for raw digital demodulation.
  • Robust API Library Support: Automate measurement routines via free, lightweight C++, C#, Python, and Java APIs.
  • MATLAB & LabVIEW Hooks: Inject live spectral data directly into laboratory computation tools for deep analysis.
