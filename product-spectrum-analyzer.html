Series

Overview

Benchtop/Handheld
Real-Time Spectrum Analyzer

PX series is the handheld real-time spectrum analyzer with 10.1-inch full touch screen and typical standby time of more than 3 h. The weight of PX model is only 1.5 kg but it covers frequency range from 9 kHz to 40 GHz, analysis bandwidth up to 100 MHz and sweep speed of up to 900 GHz/s. PX model is equipped with a standard preamplifier and used in a wide range of scenarios, such as spectrum analysis, phase noise, zero span, OBW, analog demodulation, digital demodulation. It provides engineers with a precise and reliable RF analyzer.

Compact USB
Spectrum Analyzer/Receiver

SA series from PN Technologies is designed for compact USB real-time spectrum analyzer. Based on SWaP-C and self-developed filters, the weight and size for SA series up to 40 GHz is compatible with a smart phone, suitable for embedded RF system and portable RF measurement. SA models are equipped with highly compatible APIs, empowering customers to develop their own RF system via second development.

Networked
Spectrum Analyzer/Receiver

NX series is a miniaturized network node real-time spectrum analyzer based on 1 GbE Ethernet interface and high-speed FPGA signal processing. NX model is with compact design but not simple, the RF front end contains a complete preamplifier, attenuator, multi-segment preselector. It is able to output spectrum, digital IQ stream, power detection and real-time spectrum.
