PN Technologies - Precision + Technology

PN Technologies combines compact, robust RF hardware with agile, intelligent software to empower our customers to extend the boundaries of RF. We are dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that drive performance across spectrum analysis, signal generation, and RF measurement.

What

We Do

In a world that's more dynamic and fast-paced than ever, PN Technologies strives for extending RF boundaries to break down barriers for RF innovation. At PN Technologies, we foster collaboration with our partners, actively engaging in co-creation to develop cutting-edge RF instruments up to 40 GHz and enter into market with confidence.

Our Core

Competencies

Advanced

R&D Team

Our international R&D teams span across multiple disciplines, including RF hardware, embedded systems, signal processing algorithms, and user experience design. With decades of combined experience in the test & measurement industry, we continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in compact RF instrumentation.

Factory

Our state-of-the-art SMT factory is equipped with the latest automated assembly lines, ensuring precision manufacturing and rigorous quality control. Every instrument undergoes comprehensive testing before shipment, meeting international standards for accuracy, reliability, and durability.
